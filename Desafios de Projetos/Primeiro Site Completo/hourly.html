Services MyPet

Clinic

Medical clinic aimed at companion animals with an emphasis on prevention.

Surgery

Routine surgeries: ovary-salpingo-hysterectomies, orchiectomies (castrations), mastectomies, tartarectomies and protocols.

X-ray

Diagnostic aid in the medical and surgical clinic.

Ultrasound

Auxiliary diagnostic methodology of the medical clinic with prior appointment.

Vaccines

Pediatric monitoring of your pet with routine vaccination guidelines during its development.

Laboratory

Ancillary services, such as clinical laboratory, histopathology, etc., provided by partners during business hours.

Hospitalization

Blocks with kennels and catteries for hospitalization and outpatient and postoperative care.

Pet Shop

Grooming and Parasiticidal Baths

Aesthetic grooming, medicinal baths or ecto-parasiticides, always with prior appointment.

Take-and-Bring Service

Home service

We serve at your home by appointment. Make an appointment with the receptionist.
