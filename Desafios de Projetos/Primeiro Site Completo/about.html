About the MyPet

We have been operating in the municipality of Osasco since 1910, pioneering veterinary medicine, at a time when our city was extremely lacking in this type of service.

Today, more than ever, we dedicate care to companion animals, seeking interaction between the family and the pet-friend, envisioning health, prevention and behavioral harmony.

We are a 24-hour veterinary clinic. Our team is made up of veterinarians who are specialists in different areas and types of animals, such as dogs and cats. We have all the preparation to take care of your animal with lots of love through clinical and laboratory tests, surgeries, preventive consultations, hospitalizations, vaccinations and emergency care.
